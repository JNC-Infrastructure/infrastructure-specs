### 1. Sovereign Infrastructure Standard
JNC maintains a strict standard for hardware isolation to meet the needs of the Global Corporate Market:
* **Deployment Model:** Exclusive Bare-Metal Provisioning. Zero hypervisor overhead.
* **Architecture:** Data Utility Kernels optimized for institutional processing without shared resources.
* **Egress Integrity:** Unencapsulated Layer 2/3 handoff (MTU 1500).

### 2. Global Peering & Carrier Pinning
Our affiliate governance model pins egress to verified Tier-1 carrier backbones, anchored in critical peering exchanges:
* **AS7018:** AT&T Services (Primary Path)
* **AS701:** Verizon Business / MCI (Secondary Path)
* **Optimization:** Regional PoC is engineered to peer at **Equinix MI1 (Miami)** and **Hivelocity TPA1 (Tampa).**

### 3. Verification & Operational Resilience (DORA)
This architecture is designed to meet the mandatory technical standards of the Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA). Continuous monitoring ensures path integrity:
* **DORA COMPLIANCE VERIFICATION (AUDITED):** Multi-homed carrier diversity and hardware-level isolation.
* **TELEMETRY MONITORING:** 72-hour stability validation via real-time network probes.
